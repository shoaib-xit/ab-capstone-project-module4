This capstone project transforms Amazon Prime Video's titles and credits datasets into five interactive Power BI dashboards that provide end-to-end insights into content performance, genre trends, global production, catalog freshness, and talent analytics, enabling strategic decisions for content acquisition, audience engagement, and platform growth.
The project provides a 360-degree analytical view of Amazon Prime Video's catalog, helping stakeholders optimize content investments, improve catalog freshness, expand into high-growth markets, strengthen talent partnerships, and enhance viewer engagement. By transforming raw data into interactive dashboards, it enables faster, evidence-based decision-making and supports long-term business growth in the competitive streaming industry.

**Business Problems Solved:**
- Understanding the overall composition and quality of the content library.
- Identifying the most valuable genres for future investment.
- Evaluating global production and regional growth opportunities.
- Measuring catalog freshness and identifying the need for newer content.
- Recognizing influential actors and directors who drive audience engagement.
